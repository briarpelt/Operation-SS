RULES:

Class 1 Rules

Breaking a Class 1 rule is a minor thing, and most commonly should result in any of the following, depending on severity and how often you've broken the rules.

Non-recorded warning - Limitation.

Class 2 Rules

Breaking a Class 2 rule is a more severe infraction, and most commonly should result in any of the following, depending on severity and how often you've broken the rules.

Recorded warning - 7-day ban.

Class 3 Rules

Breaking a Class 3 rule is a very serious infraction, and most commonly should result in any of the following, depending on severity and how often you've broken the rules.

3-day ban - Permanent ban
